## 原文摘要

与仅在查询时从原始文档检索信息不同，LLM 会逐步构建并维护一个持久的维基——一种介于您和原始资料之间的结构化、相互链接的 Markdown 文件集合。
Instead of just retrieving from raw documents at query time, the LLM **incrementally builds and maintains a persistent wiki** — a structured, interlinked collection of markdown files that sits between you and the raw sources.

这是关键区别：维基是一个持久且不断累积的工件。交叉引用已经存在。矛盾已被标记。
This is the key difference: **the wiki is a persistent, compounding artifact.** The cross-references are already there.
